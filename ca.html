HMO plans have no out-of-network coverage. Any care received outside California costs 100% out of pocket. If you need coverage in a second state, use the National Plans.
AVN plans (CA3, CA5) use the Aetna Vital Network — a narrower provider set with lower premiums. Always verify your doctors are in the AVN network before enrolling.
